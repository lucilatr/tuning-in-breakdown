P1 — Lead Characters
Zoe
Lead
14 years old — Daughter of Miles and Kay. Emerging artist and composer. Alias: Zowie Stardust.
P1 Full Kit + Variant
Images34
Clips3–5
Physical Description
Contemporary cool style. Multicolored hair. Neo-pop-punk bedazzled look that screams future rock star. As a little kid: wild clothes and wild hair. Her own headphones — neon pink, chunky, and covered in gems — 100% Zoe.
Personality
Creative, passionate about music since birth. Grief over her father makes her defensive, irritable, and shut down. Strong temper — she breaks Jade's nose with one punch. Enormous emotional depth. Her arc: from emotional silence to finding her own voice.
Skills
Guitar, sampler, MIDI controller, production laptop, drums. Creates electronic music blended across genres. Combines sounds from every genre into a style that is entirely her own.
Wardrobe Variations
  • Neo-pop-punk bedazzled — multicolored hair, present-day vlog look
  • Hoodie + blanket — night in the living room, doomscrolling
  • Backpack slung low — school scenes
  • Dad's vintage can headphones — attic sessions, World of Music
  • Neon pink bedazzled headphones — finale, her own identity

Fuse
Metal Spirit · P1 Lead
The Screamin' Demon — Metal music spirit of Metal Mountain. Zoe's guitar duel opponent.
P1 Full Kit + Variant
Images34
Clips3–5
Physical Description
Little gremlin music spirit with wild electric-blue hair, studded black leather vest, lightning bolt shades, and giant platform boots (he clearly needs them). His voice has built-in reverb. Makes a full pyrotechnics entrance — levitating from stalagmites.
Personality
All theatrics on the outside — genuine on the inside. Knew Miles from his garage band phase. Can shred like Eddie Van Halen. Loses a guitar duel to Zoe and is stunned. Reluctantly gives her his respect — and his Lead Zeppelin.

P2 — Core Cast
Miles
Father / Emotional Engine
20s at the start — Zoe's father, Kay's husband. Passes away before the story's present.
P2 Full Kit + 4 Variants
Images55
Clips3
Physical Description
Man in his twenties, scruffy cool look. Always surrounded by instruments and scattered sheet music. Wears classic can headphones. When ill: thinner, eyes closed, shallow breathing.
Personality
Present, tender, and playful father. Middle school music teacher with genuine calling. Patient, inspiring, full of humor. Never famous in the real world — enormously beloved in the World of Music.
Legacy
Guitarist, pianist, and songwriter. Met Kay in a garage band. Spent years working on "A Song for Zoe" — deliberately left unfinished as a gift for his daughter. Has his own neighborhood in the World of Music: "Miles' Place."
Wardrobe Variations
  • Casual scruffy musician — flannel, worn jeans, vintage tees — attic studio
  • Hospital scrubs — nursery scene when Zoe is born
  • Sick / bedridden — pale, thin, minimal clothing — illness scenes
  • Garage band era — late 90s grunge, flannel — flashback with Kay

Kay
Mother
20s at the start, adult in the present — Zoe's mother, Miles's widow.
P2 Full Kit + 4 Variants
Images55
Clips3
Physical Description
Young woman at the start, always in hospital scrubs. Pregnant in the opening scene. In the present, she's an adult woman holding the family together on her own.
Personality
Practical, hardworking, structured. Seems to "keep going like nothing happened" after Miles dies — which deeply frustrates Zoe. In reality devastated: seen crying in private. Has a beautiful, clear, and gentle voice. Finds music as the way back to her daughter.
Wardrobe Variations
  • Hospital scrubs — present-day, work scenes, morning routines
  • Casual maternity wear — pregnancy scenes at the start
  • Informal home clothes — domestic scenes, late nights
  • Garage band era — late 90s casual — flashback with Miles

Pip-Pop
Music Spirit / Guide
Sir Pipalonious-Pop-Bop-Boomzarella the Third, Prince of Pop — Music spirit and Miles's old friend.
